Vision

AI should be useful, understandable, and reachable.

My vision for AnemiaVision AI is to support early screening where lab access is delayed or limited. The product is designed around fast image capture, clear confidence, Grad-CAM visual explanation, patient history, analytics, and PDF reporting.

This is not a replacement for clinical testing. It is a decision-support layer that can help people identify risk earlier and seek confirmatory medical care sooner.
