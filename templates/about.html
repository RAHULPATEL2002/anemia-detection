Technology overview

How the AI works

AnemiaVision AI uses a pretrained EfficientNet-B3 convolutional network to analyze smartphone images of the eye conjunctiva and fingernail bed. The model looks for color and texture patterns linked to reduced hemoglobin appearance, then produces a binary screening result with confidence and Grad-CAM explainability.

Clinical caveats

Limitations and disclaimer

  • This application is designed for screening support, not definitive diagnosis.
  • Lighting, blur, motion, skin tone variation, and camera quality can affect predictions.
  • Visual estimates of hemoglobin are approximate and should be confirmed with laboratory testing.
  • Patients with symptoms or high-risk results should be referred for professional medical review.
